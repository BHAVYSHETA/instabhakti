Navadha Bhakti

Nine Bhaktis Presentation Section

Each card below is ready for your own PPT link. If no link is added, the page will show a small message instead of opening a file.

01

Shravanam

Devotion through listening

Hearing divine names, teachings, and sacred stories with full attention and devotion.

02

Kirtanam

Devotion through singing

Singing the glories of the divine through bhajans, kirtans, and joyful remembrance.

03

Smaranam

Devotion through remembrance

Keeping the divine in the heart and mind through constant inner remembrance.

04

Pada Sevanam

Devotion through service

Serving the feet of the Lord with humility, care, and sacred action.

05

Archanam

Devotion through worship

Offering worship, flowers, and reverence through rituals and sacred intention.

06

Vandanam

Devotion through prayer

Bowing with gratitude and expressing heartfelt prayers to the divine.

07

Dasyam

Devotion through servitude

Living as a devoted servant of the divine with loyalty and discipline.

08

Sakhyam

Devotion through friendship

Building a loving friendship with the divine through trust and closeness.

09

Atma Nivedanam

Devotion through surrender

Offering the entire self to the divine in complete surrender and faith.
